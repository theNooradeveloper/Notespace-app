FAQs

What makes this app unique?

Our app combines powerful note-taking features with creative tools like a canvas, making it a one-stop solution for productivity and creativity.

How do I customize my notes?

You can personalize your notes by choosing from a variety of colors and fonts. This allows you to make your notes visually appealing and organized.

Is my data safe and secure?

Absolutely! We use Google login and Firebase for secure authentication and cloud storage, ensuring your data is protected and accessible only to you.

What is the purpose of the canvas feature?

The canvas is designed for brainstorming and creativity. It lets you draw, sketch, and create visual ideas, which you can save and manage alongside your notes.

Will offline functionality be available?

Yes, offline functionality is a planned feature. It will allow you to access and manage your notes even without an internet connection.
